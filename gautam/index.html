ABOUT US

Gautam Color Lab was established in 1987 at Shri Dungargarh (Bikaner). In the beginning its name was Gautam Studio. There we have provided indoor and outdoor photography, video shooting, passport photos etc.

In 1990's gautam studio's another branch opened in bikaner. It's name was Gautam Color Lab. Here we have provided best quality products in various formats such as wedding albums, passport photos, modelling photos, photo book, photo calendar etc. Our main motive have decorate your life beautiful moments in photography.

In 2018 We have shifted our showroom to near by place. Now we have added some products like mug printing t-shirt printing, photo frames and pre wedding shoot also and many more as per customers requirements. We have venus company products such as album, photos, cut outs etc. In later 2018 We have starting products like banners, flex etc.
